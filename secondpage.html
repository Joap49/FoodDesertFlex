How can we help?

  1. Find local food deserts. To find a food desert near you, enter your zip code in the USDA’s Food Desert Locator: ers.usda.gov/data/fooddesert.
  2. Volunteer at community gardens.
  3. Donate fresh produce. Find food banks, soup kitchens, and other organizations fighting hunger through Food Pantries or Feeding America: foodpantries.org feedingamerica.org/find-your- local-foodbank.
